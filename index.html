About lostinbreak

Formerly known as Ubered Sandvich, lostinbreak has been producing electronic music since 2014. Inspired by the sounds of many genres such as breakcore and wave, he creates a distinct sound full of warm ambient tones and dark melodies woven together. He also frequently branches out to new genres, trying to find new sounds and stylings to incorporate into his music.
